CHALLENGE OVERVIEW

Are you a student innovator with groundbreaking ideas to transform healthcare in Africa? The AFRETEC-UNILAG Innovation-in-Health Challenge 2025 is calling on multidisciplinary teams from Nigerian universities to develop scalable solutions for Africa’s most pressing healthcare challenges.

Focus Areas: Digital Health & Telemedicine, Maternal & Child Health, AI & Data Analytics, Affordable Medical Devices, and Mental Health & Well-being.

Why Participate?

By joining the challenge, you stand to gain:

  • Proof of Concept Funding: Up to ₦500,000 for the Top 10 teams.
  • Mentorship: Guidance from industry health experts.
  • Cash Awards: Up to ₦4.5 million for the Top 3 innovations.
  • Industry Linkage: Opportunities to connect with leading healthcare organizations.
